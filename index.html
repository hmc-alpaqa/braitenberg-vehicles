How to Use

Adding a Vehicle

To add a vehicle, select a vehicle in the dropdown and click the Add Vehicle button. Then, move your mouse to where you wish to place the vehicle. Use the left and right arrow keys to adjust the angle. You can keep adding vehicles while the Add Vehicle button is pressed. To change the vehicle being added, reselect the vehicle in the dropdown. If you wish to stop adding vehicles, click the Add Vehicle button again.

Adding a Source

To add a source, enter the desired intensity of the source and click the Add Source button. Then, move your mouse to where you wish to place the source. You can keep adding sources while the Add source button is pressed. To change the intensity of the source being added, enter the new desired intensity in the input box. If you wish to stop adding sources, click the Add Source button again.

Removing a Source

To remove a source, click the Remove Source button. Then, click on the source you wish to remove. You can keep removing sources while the Remove Source button is pressed. If you wish to stop removing sources, click the Remove Source button again.
